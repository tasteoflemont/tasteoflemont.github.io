About

Food, Music, Family Fun

The Taste of Lemont is a fun, family friendly event that is made possible in part by Visionary Sponsor Citgo. The event will feature some of the best food and beverages the Lemont community can offer, a substantial split the pot raffle, and an 8 p.m. concert from one of Chicagoland’s top bands - Mike & Joe!

Band

Mike & Joe

Mike & Joe is one of the nation’s top rock cover bands. Its sets typically feature hits from artists ranging from U2 and Maroon 5, to Justin Timberlake and the Dave Matthews Band. While its shows most often feature hits from the 90s through today, Mike & Joe throws in songs from classic rock royalty such as The Eagles and Fleetwood Mac as well. The LHSEF is excited to welcome Mike & Joe’s stable of loyal fans to the Taste of Lemont in 2017!

- Check out Mike & Joe at www.mikeandjoe.com

Proceeds

Proceeds from “The Taste of Lemont” benefit the Lemont High School Educational Foundation, whose mission is to partner with the community to make lasting improvements to Lemont High School that will enhance the total educational experience for all of its students. The Foundation funds and supports programs and opportunities that aid students’ growth, both academically and as citizens of the Lemont community. The Foundation is a not-for-profit charitable organization. All gifts to the Foundation are tax-deductible to the extent provided by the law.
