About

Food, Music, Family Fun

The Taste of Lemont is a fun, family friendly event that is made possible in part by Visionary Sponsor Citgo. The event will feature some of the best food and beverages the Lemont community can offer, a substantial split the pot raffle, with the night's music provided by Split Decision and Mike and Joe.



Band

Split Decision

Split Decision is a four piece band that will have you on your feet! Awesome rhythm section, ripping guitar leads and "wow" lead vocals. You might recognize them from another band, and well... that is why they are the Split Decision Band! The LHSEF is excited to welcome Split Decision and their loyal fans to the Taste of Lemont in 2022! - Check out Split Decision https://www.facebook.com/Split-Decision-Band-319236528117697/

Band

Mike and Joe

Mike & Joe is a modern rock cover band widely regarded by its peers and the industry as one of the very best sounding cover bands in the nation. The bulk of the setlist includes popular rock from the 90’s through today, but occasionally they’ll throw in some tunes from the 70’s and 80’s as well. Bring your shoes, bring your glasses. The LHSEF is excited to welcome Mike and Joe and their loyal fans to the Taste of Lemont in 2022!

- Check out Mike and Joe https://www.mikeandjoe.com/

Proceeds

Proceeds from the "Taste of Lemont” benefit the Lemont High School Educational Foundation, whose mission is to partner with the community to make lasting improvements to Lemont High School that will enhance the total educational experience for all of its students. The Foundation funds and supports programs and opportunities that aid students’ growth, both academically and as citizens of the Lemont community. The Foundation is a not-for-profit charitable organization. All gifts to the Foundation are tax-deductible to the extent provided by the law.
